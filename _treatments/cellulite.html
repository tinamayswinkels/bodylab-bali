What are the different stages of cellulite?

CELLULITE STAGE 1: When pinched gently between the forefinger and thumb, the skin shows a slight ‘orange-peel’ effect’.

CELLULITE STAGE 2: The skin feels thicker and slightly tender if pinched gently between the forefingers and thumb. You can definitely see an orange-peel effect.

CELLULITE STAGE 3: Without pinching, you can observe an orange-peel effect. Fluid retention is permanently present and it can cause discomfort in everyday life.

CELLULITE STAGE 4: Without pinching, you can observe a "mattress surface" effect: the skin is visibly puckered in, creating visible irregularities in its surface.
