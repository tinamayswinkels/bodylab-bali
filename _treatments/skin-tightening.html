Skin Tightening Treatments in Bali

 

We use cutting-edge aesthetic machines to help you create the tight, toned body you've always dreamed of. Our experienced therapists can create a personalised treatment plan that will slim and sculpt your body with lasting results and minimal downtime. 

What are the most effective skin tightening treatments at Body Lab Bali?

At Body Lab Bali we work exclusively with the latest, most efficient technology:

-Endymed Radiofrequency, which heats the dermis, boosting collagen production and  elastin synthesis, improving oxygen levels in the skin which results in softer fine lines, lifted, firmer skin on your face, and tighter, more radiant skin in your body, after only one treatment.

-High Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HiFU) such as Liftera, to encourage the production of collagen, which results in visibly tighter, firmer skin with fewer wrinkles. HiFU is also fantastic to lift cheeks, eyebrows and eyelids, tighten decolletage and sagging skin on the neck and enhance the jawline definition.

How many treatments do I need and what should I expect during a session?

With Endymed, you will see results from the first session and we suggest having 4-7 treatments, once per week. This treatment is fantastic as there is no downtime or pain involved during your sessions.

With Liftera HiFU you can experience very mild pain as we work on SMAS level (3 - 4.5 mm below the skin surface, where the facial muscles are connected to the upper layer of the skin, which is also the layer that plastic surgeons manually pulls during a face lift)

Treatment can be done once every 30-90 days for up to 3-4 times. And the fabulous results can last for up to 2 years.
