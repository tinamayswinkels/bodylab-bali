Platelet Rich Plasma Facials is one of our most popular Anti-Aging and skin-glowing treatments.

At Body Lab Bali we use only the safest procedures and all injectables are administered by an experienced medical professional.

 

What is PRP Facial and how does it work?

The vampire facial, also known as the Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP facial), includes drawing blood from your arm, separating the platelets, and then injecting them into your skin via microneedling injections.

The doctor runs the needle pen over your face while squirting the platelets into the mini injection holes.

Since the platelets are high in growth hormone, and the microneedling stimulates the skin to regenerate, the entire procedure is meant to aid cell turnover.

What type of results can I expect?

Platelet-Rich Plasma treatments are well known for:

  • Increasing collagen production

  • Reducing fine lines and wrinkles

  • Firming and tightening skin

  • Improving moisture retention

  • Enhancing skin tone and texture

  • Reducing pore size and hyperpigmentation

  • Minimising broken capillaries and spider veins

  • Improving skin texture and appearance of scars

How many sessions do I need to get results?

Ideally 2-5 sessions, once every 4-6 weeks to obtain and maintain the best results, but after the very first treatment you will be able to appreciate a radiant glow and visible improvement in your skin's firmness and tone.
