What are Facial Fillers?

Dermal fillers are gel-like substances that are injected beneath the skin to help diminish facial lines and restore volume and fullness in the face.

At Body Lab Bali we use only the leading world brands of dermal fillers, including Juvederm Voluma, Juvederm Volift, Belotero Intense, Belotero Balance and many more!

Are the results permanent?

Depending on the type of filler, the effects can last anywhere from six months to two years.
