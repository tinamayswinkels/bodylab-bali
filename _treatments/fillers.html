What are Facial Fillers?

Dermal fillers are gel-like substances that are injected beneath the skin to help diminish facial lines and restore volume and fullness in the face.

At Body Lab Bali we use only the leading world brands of dermal fillers, including Juvederm Voluma, Juvederm Volift, Belotero Intense, Belotero Balance and many more!

Are the results permanent?

Depending on the type of filler, the effects can last anywhere from six months to two years.

Any precautions before the treatment?

 -24hrs prior to your treatment: avoid alcoholic beverages

-2 days prior to your treatment: Avoid waxing, bleaching, or hair removal cream and any topical Anti-Aging products such as Tretinoin (Retin-A), Retinol, Retinoids, Glycolic Acid, etc.

-One week prior: avoid medication such as Aspirin, Ibuprofen, and Aleve as they’re blood thinners, and ideally avoid supplements such as gingko biloba, St. John’s Wort,, primrose oil, ginseng, garlic and Vitamin E

Any precautions after treatment?

We recommend to avoid alcohol, strenuous exercise, and intense heat (such as saunas, sunbathing, etc) in the treated area as it may result in additional bruising or swelling.

To minimise bruising, you can apply Arnica or Bromelain creams and ice the treated area. (Eating fresh pineapple also helps!)

To alleviate swelling, you can use any over the counter antihistamine for a few days.
