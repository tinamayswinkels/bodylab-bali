Face Lifting & Contouring Treatments in Bali

What are Face Lifting and Contouring face treatments?

At Body Lab Bali we work only with the latest technology to help you reduce the signs of ageing, remove excess fat tissue and tone the facial muscles. We offer different treatments to lift and highlight your best features and improve skin firmness and tone so you can enjoy a youthful and fresh appearance.

Our signature and best seller treatments are Thermolift, Transion, Fat Freezing, Isogei Face Lift, Endymed Skin Tightening and Liftera A. 

How many sessions do I need to see results??

You will see an immediate improvement in your skin appearance with most of our treatments, and you will see visible, more dramatic results after 4-8 sessions. 

Generally, with all Face Lifting and Contouring face treatments  20% - 40% of the results can be seen immediately after the first treatment, and  3-6 months later the final result is visible.

Are the results permanent?

Most of our treatments will give you incredible results that will last 3-9 months, depending on your lifestyle and skin care routine.

We strongly recommend to support the treatment results by following a healthy diet, avoiding smoking, alcohol or sun exposure and drinking plenty of water on a daily basis.

For all our treatments that are non-surgical and non-invasive, we suggest for you to have 1-2 sessions every couple of months to maintain the results.
