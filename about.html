About Body Lab

YOUR PREMIERE DESTINATION FOR WELLNESS AND BEAUTY

Located in the heart of Seminyak, Body Lab Bali is a rejuvenation and slimming spa that offers the latest in non-surgical cosmetic treatments - from anti-ageing and weight loss to botox and dermal fillers. 

Proudly Russian owned and managed, we're committed to the wellness of our patients and only use state-of-the-art aesthetic technology and premium imported products to achieve lasting results.

With us, you can be sure that you'll receive high quality treatments in a safe and hygienic environment. Our team of medical professionals have years of experience in administering cosmetic treatments, adhering to the strictest international standards.

 

Contact us to discuss a custom treatment plan that suits your needs.

 

WE'RE DEDICATED TO QUALITY, SAFETY AND PROFESSIONALISM 

Our team of medical professionals have years of experience in administering world class anti-ageing and slimming treatments.

We only use cutting-edge aesthetic technology and dermatologist-approved products to ensure the quality of all procedures.

We’ll customize our treatments around the results you want to achieve, your budget and the amount of time you have available. We will never try to up-sell you.

 

 

INJECTABLE REJUVENATION TREATMENTS

 

We’re one of the premiere destinations in Bali for injectable rejuvenation treatments like Botox, fillers, skin boosters, rejuvenation IV infusions and fat burning injections.

These delicate procedures are administered by an experienced medical professional, using only imported medical-grade products.

The brands we use for our injectables include Juvederm Voluma, Juvederm Volift, Belotero Intense, Belotero Balance and Allergen (from the USA).
